  • 01. Sorting through Domestic Issue

    Lewis Fredericks is proud to only use horn that is a byproduct of animal domestication. It is both a renewable and a sustainable practice.

    It is essential the horn is of the utmost quality in order to create frames of distinction; our selection process is testament to this.

    We immediately discard around a fifth of the horn we receive due to imperfections and sizing issues.

  • 02. Horn to Horn Plate

Lewis Fredericks frames are made from real horn, not a manufactured imitation. Each individual pair of Lewis Fredericks is a completely unique, one of a kind piece.

Every frame has a unique appearance, with naturally-occurring variations in color, striations and horn pattern. For this reason we do not operate a traditional online store.

We encourage you to visit our stockists to choose a frame, or alternatively, to get in touch by leaving your email address. We will contact you with the range that is currently in stock, and assist you in choosing a frame that fits your taste.
