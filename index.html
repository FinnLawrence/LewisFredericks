HORN

  • ABOUT / CARE
  • ONE OF ONE

Lewis Fredericks Eyewear offers time-honoured silhouettes fashioned from horn, the previously wasted by-product of domesticated Buffalo, Ram and Ox.

With mass production and little technique now a calling card of the eyewear industry, Lewis Fredericks instead pays tribute to the rich history of eyewear’s past, through recycling the traditionally used material of horn and expert hand craftsmanship.

Horn is a natural fibre; its integrity, pattern and structure cannot be replicated, making each individual pair of Lewis Fredericks a completely unique, one of a kind piece.

The fibre of horn carries with it properties that outweigh any synthetic counterpart. Lightweight and durable, each pair will gracefully flex and gradually mould to the contours of the wearer’s face.

Aware of modern tastes and needs, Lewis Fredericks reinvigorates past design by employing technological advancements including high quality hinges and Italian polarised lenses from Mazzucchelli.

A fine balance of design, technique and craftsmanship, Lewis Fredericks combines the best from the past to create classic, contemporary eyewear.

CARE

Horn is one of the strongest and most durable optical materials. Your Lewis Fredericks will respond well to being outdoors and exposed to the elements. However, due to being a natural fibre be sure to polish them with the included wax. We recommend applying this or any mineral or petroleum based oil twice a year to nourish the horn and prevent it from drying.

To apply; buff a small amount of the wax into your frame with the microfiber cleaning cloth or rag, paying special attention to the temples and frame edges. Buff the frame until the wax has completely dissipated and the horn appears revitalised.

Your Lewis Fredericks will grow with you in both form and feel, care for them well and you will enjoy our vision for many years.

Lewis Fredericks has attempted to categorize the horn colouring in Schedule One in order to provide guidance. Light horn is the most variable of the Lewis Fredericks horn categories and it is in this category that we advise customers to read the following before making a purchase:
Because buffalo horn is a natural material, every Lewis Fredericks Frame will have a unique appearance, with naturally-occurring variations in color, striations and horn pattern. Showroom samples and photographic reproductions can give an example of the product’s appearance but they may not be representative of the full range of colour striation and horn variations which can occur in the product itself. The images on this website are provided for reference only and should not be used as the sole basis for choosing a particular style of colouring.
